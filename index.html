About

Dr. Baiyun Chen is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science within the Andrew F. Brimmer College of Business and Information Science at Tuskegee University.

Her research focuses on trustworthy and robust machine learning, deep learning and large language models, learning with noisy and imbalanced data, imbalanced and multiclass classification, and AI applications in healthcare and cybersecurity.

Dr. Chen has contributed to research on label noise learning, robust imbalanced classification, large language models, malware detection, credit card fraud detection, building energy analytics, and biomedical AI. She is also a Co-PI on a National Science Foundation CyberTraining project supporting AI and cybersecurity education for cyberinfrastructure.

Research Interests

  • Deep learning and large language models
  • Learning with noisy and imbalanced data
