About

Dr. Baiyun Chen is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Tuskegee University.

Her research focuses on Trustworthy and Secure AI, with an emphasis on developing reliable, robust, and secure machine learning systems for real-world applications, particularly in high-impact domains such as cybersecurity and healthcare. Her work spans large language models, robust machine learning, uncertainty-aware AI, and AI security.

Her long-term goal is to develop AI systems that remain trustworthy and resilient under real-world challenges, including uncertainty, noisy and imperfect data, adversarial threats, and evolving environments.
