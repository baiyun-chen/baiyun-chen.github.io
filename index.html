About

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Tuskegee University's Andrew F. Brimmer College of Business and Information Science.

My work develops robust machine learning methods for complex classification scenarios and connects those methods to practical AI systems in healthcare, cybersecurity, education, fraud detection, and energy analytics.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy and robust machine learning
  • Deep learning and large language models
  • Learning with noisy and imbalanced data
  • Imbalanced and multiclass classification
  • AI applications in healthcare and cybersecurity
